# Netflix EDA SQL Project

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Netflix content catalog using SQL — uncovering trends in movies, TV shows, genres, countries, and audience ratings.

---

## Business Problem

Netflix wants to better understand its content library to support strategic decisions around content acquisition and investment. Additionally, knowing which regions produce the most content helps guide international expansion strategies.

---

## Business Objectives

1. Understand the distribution of Movies vs. TV Shows on Netflix.
2. Identify which countries contribute the most content.
3. Determine which genres dominate the Netflix catalog.
4. Analyze how Netflix content has grown over time.
5. Understand the distribution of audience content ratings.
6. Analyze typical movie duration and number of seasons for TV shows.
7. Identify the most frequent directors and actors.
